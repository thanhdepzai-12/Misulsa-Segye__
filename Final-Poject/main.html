Vangogh

Vincent Van Gogh (1853 - 1890) was an artist with exceptional talent. Influenced by the Impressionist painters and during that period he developed his own instinctive, improvisational style. Van Gogh became one of the most famous artists of the twentieth century and played an important role in the development of modern art.

Picasso

Pablo Ruiz Picasso commonly known as Pablo Picasso or Picasso is a Spanish painter and sculptor. Picasso is considered one of the most prominent artists of the 20th century, he and Georges Braque are the two founders of cubism in painting and sculpture. He is one of the 10 greatest painters in the top 200 greatest visual artists in the world of the 20th century
